Choose exactly what they see.
Nothing more.

  • Toggle visibility per tab — no complex permissions
  • Rename tabs for the client-facing view
  • Internal tabs are invisible, not just locked
  • Update visibility any time — link stays the same

Client Comments & Review

Feedback on the work. No account. No Slack thread.

External reviewers can leave comments directly on shared blocks — no account needed, just a name. Their feedback syncs back to your workspace and triggers internal notifications. Review happens where the work lives.

  • No login required for external commenters
  • Comments sync to your workspace in real time
  • Internal notifications link directly to the comment
  • Client identities show as "Client" — your team stays private
  • Works on blocks, gallery images, cards, and files

Approvals & Sign-Off

Approved means approved. The team knows instantly.

Clients can review work and mark deliverables as approved. The approval state flows back into your project so the team knows when something is locked and ready to ship — no chasing confirmation over email.

  • Approve button on any shared deliverable
  • Approval state syncs back to your workspace instantly
  • Approved items auto-lock — no accidental edits
  • Approval history logged with timestamp and reviewer

File Delivery & Collection

Share files out. Collect deliverables back in.

Share files, galleries, PDFs, and videos through the public page. Clients download what they need and upload deliverables back — all tracked within the project, not buried in email attachments.

  • Share any file type through the public project page
  • Clients upload revisions directly back to the project
  • Uploads are tracked and timestamped inside the project
  • No email attachments, no Google Drive permissions chaos

Anonymized & Safe

Scoped. Signed.
Secure by design.

Client-facing comments show as "Client" — internal author identities stay private. Rate limiting, scoped access, and signed URLs keep shared pages secure and performant. Analytics track who's viewing what.

  • Client identities anonymized — no internal names exposed
  • Rate limiting prevents abuse of public links
  • Signed URLs expire and can be revoked at any time
  • Scoped access — external viewers only see what you share
  • Analytics: views, time on page, files downloaded
