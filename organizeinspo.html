Core Attributes

Every item in Trak — tasks, timeline events, table rows, cards, even content blocks — carries the same five core attributes: status, priority, assignee, due date, and tags. That means everything rolls up into one view. One place to see the state of every product launch, every campaign, every deliverable across your entire operation.

Five properties. Everything unified.

One model for your entire workspace.

When every item shares the same properties, you can filter, group, and aggregate across your entire operation — not just within a single project or tool.

The Everything Page

A workspace-wide view that aggregates every trackable item into one surface. Filter by status, priority, assignee, due date, tags, project, or type. Switch between table and board layouts. Save views and return to them.

Tasks That Go Deep

Run workloads in list, board, or table view. Group by status, priority, assignee, or due date. Drill into subtasks and detail cards without leaving the page. See task distribution at a glance with rollup bars.

Database

Tables As Databases

Rich field types — status, priority, tags, person, date, relation, rollup, and formula. View the same data as table, board, timeline, calendar, list, or gallery. Filter, sort, group, pin, and save views.

Planning

Timelines With Teeth

Gantt-style planning with dependencies, auto-scheduling, milestones, and baselines for plan-vs-actual tracking. Zoom from day to year. Shift one date and everything downstream adjusts.

Analytics

Charts From Live Data

Bar, line, pie, and doughnut charts generated from tasks, timelines, tables, and cards. Keep them live for daily standups or freeze a snapshot for the board deck. The AI can build one from a question.

Calendar

A Calendar That Knows Everything

Tasks, project deadlines, timeline events, and Google Calendar — merged into one view across month, week, and day. Click any date to see the workload and create tasks directly.
